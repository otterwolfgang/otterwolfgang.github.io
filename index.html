about me.

My name is Wolfgang Otter and I am a Data Scientist at Wien Energie, where I work on models used in all stages of product development and management, and on the general analytics strategy as it relates to the customer lifecycle.

I have had the incredible opportunity of being able to fluidly transition between product development and analytics and I was able to see the best of both worlds. Product ownership can profit immensely from a partnering analytical framework that is used in all stages from conceptualization to reporting, otherwise risking to become a quest in the dark. On the other hand, analytics without a clear purpose can oftentimes (not always, of course) lead to charts and dashboards that may shine but aren’t ever put to good use (other than to maybe briefly entertain management).

In both worlds I mostly occupied the role of project lead/product owner, bringing solutions from concept to reality and trying to combine my knowledge in both fields.

This site is built with Jekyll. Most of the inspiration for the general layout comes from Zachary R Newton’s great tutorial on building a website. The banner image is by Amy Hirschi.
